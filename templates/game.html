The game

How to Play

The game is set within a 3D virtual environment.

Players can navigate their way through the levels using virtual touchscreen joysticks used in contemporary games.

Instructions on what to do are given throughout the two levels

Making the game

This game is a collaboration between North Down Museum, Bangor Grammar, Nerve Centre and BT48.com.

Pupils from Bangor Grammar were involved in all aspects of the game from conception, level design and structure.
